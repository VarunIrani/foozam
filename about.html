Food Detection and Classification

Foozam is a Progressive Web Application which recognizes food images and classifies them. It tells the name, nutrients, and recipes of the food item it recognizes. We provide all the solutions for the problems related to food in one place

Foozam users do not have to go through surfing the Internet for the nutrients, recipe or information that the food item possesses.

Exceptional Web Solutions

Our mission is to provide accurate image classification and recognition. We want to develop the strongest network on the web for foodies and interconnect them to each other. Our aim is to make the strongest network for the development and encouragement of food technology

Satisfy Our Users

The goal of our application is to fully satisfy our users with 100% accurate results and providing them with all the information they require related to food at one place at one platform. Our goal is to make our application the ultimate source of all the information related to food.
